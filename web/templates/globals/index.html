Locator-IO

On this subpage, you can play a little game, where you need to find a Location, using only satelite imagery. Amazingly, you can do so against somebody else. Do to this you need to Create or Join a Lobby, press Ready and then simply play. The rules are simple, click at the location you think is correct, and at the end of a round your are awarded points. Currently you can play a few certain regions/city sizes. However the points awareded are not influenced by the size of the area. This will be addressed in further releases.
