Locator.io

Play alone or against friends. Purpose of the game is to locate a city with only satellite imagery as a base.
Get points based on distance to actual location. Most points win.
Visually very basic. Not optimized for Phones, yet.

Cool because:

  • Fun to play!
  • Written in Go
  • Uses Websockets
  • Generates MapboxVectorTiles on the fly thanks to Orb
  • Generates knowledge
